Group classes

YOGA

Discover classes that will propel you forward on your journey to body and mind harmony. We look forward to welcoming you in our studios in Vinohrady and Karlín. During the summer season, we also organize outdoor yoga in the fresh air at attractive locations throughout Prague.

BATTLE

Battle is a transformative experience that pushes you beyond your limits. The combination of boxing techniques and strength training, coupled with the immersive atmosphere, creates an unparalleled workout that challenges your body and mind. It's not about being the best in the world; it's about discovering the best version of yourself.

BOOTY

Booty group class will help you push yourself to the very edge of your physical capabilities and achieve great results. It's a sophisticated concept that offers a wide range of variations, intensities and motivational training programs, and music will ensure an unforgettable experience that manifests itself with every step. The class is focused on building strength and shaping the lower body. Climbing the stairs has never been more fun.

    About NextLevel

    Our fitness center blends modern design with the latest fitness technology, creating the perfect space for your workout. Alongside a full range of equipment, you'll find a cozy lounge with a Fuel Bar, and a unique atmosphere that elevates your fitness experience. Whether you're taking part in group classes or working out solo, our space is designed to inspire and help you take your fitness to the next level.

    NextLevel is a unique fitness space in Prague, designed to help you unlock your full fitness potential. We prioritize your health and overall exercise experience above all else,ensuring that performance is always balanced with well-being. Every aspect of our gym is meticulously planned, down to the smallest detail. Our flagship location serves as a welcoming environment where both beginners and professional athletes can thrive.
